The content of our work reflects several key concepts covered in class, including the ethical considerations surrounding AI development, the potential impacts of technological advancement, and speculative scenarios about the future. By portraying a world where AI outlive humans and inherit the Earth, we explore questions about the role of technology in shaping our destiny and the long-term consequences of our choices. Additionally, the narrative touches on themes of social inequality, as only the wealthiest individuals are able to escape Earth, leaving others behind to face the consequences of environmental collapse. Overall, our work serves a dark, humorous turn of a possible future while also shedding light on the intricate relationship between humans, AI, and our planet.

Creator: Genie Hou, Hana Selmani, Hamad Alshamsi, Majid Ibrahim
